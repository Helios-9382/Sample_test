Our Little Love Questionnaire ❤️

Answer honestly… I'm curious what you'll say 😌

1. When you see me after a long time, what is the first thing that comes to your mind?

2. What is one thing I do that makes you smile even when you try not to?

3. What is the most annoying thing I do that you secretly find cute?

4. When I try to flirt with you, what is your usual reaction?

5. What is one moment with me that stayed in your mind longer than it should have?

6. If we had a whole day with no one around, what would we most likely end up doing?

7. What is something you like about me but will probably never say directly?

8. What is one thing you would never change about me?

9. What is one thing you think I understand about you better than others?

10. What do you think I love the most about you?

11. What is something you think I do only because I care about you?

12. What is one thing you wish I would do more often?

13. What is one random memory of us that still makes you smile?

14. If you had to describe me in three words, what would they be?

15. If you could ask me one question and I had to answer honestly, what would you ask?

16. What is something about us that makes our relationship different from others?

17. What is something small between us that feels special only to you?

18. What is something small I do that makes you unexpectedly happy?

19. If you had to predict our future in one sentence, what would it be?

20. What is the first thing you noticed about me when we started talking?

Your answers will be kept safely… but I will definitely read them carefully.
